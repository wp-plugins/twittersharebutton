== Description ==

The simplest way to add a Twitter Share Button to posts on your WordPress blog.  Just activate the plugin and start getting Twitter tweets for posts on your blog.  People will be sharing your posts on Twitter before you know it and giving you more traffic and readers on your site.

No complicated setup or mess of settings.  Just activate and you're done!

You have the option to add the Twitter Share Button at the top of your posts or at the bottom.  This setting is available on the "Reading" page of your settings in your WordPress admin panel. It will default to the bottom of the post.

The easiest way to add a Twitter Share Button to your site!

== Installation ==

1. Upload `twitter-share-button.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

OR

Just grab it from the built in plugin directory and choose "Install now", hit ok, then select the "Activate Plugin" link.
